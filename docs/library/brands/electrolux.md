# Electrolux appliance care and service notes

Original brand-oriented guidance (not sourced from any manufacturer manual).
Nothing here overrides the safety interrupt or the deterministic troubleshooting
trees in `app/knowledge/*.yaml`.

## About the brand at Sears

Electrolux is a full-line European-rooted brand that Sears sells and services:
laundry, refrigerators, dishwashers, ranges, wall ovens, and cooktops.
Electrolux is also the parent of Frigidaire, so the two brands frequently share
parts families and control conventions.

## Service notes

- Electrolux units typically show letter/digit error codes on the control
  panel; ask the caller to read the code exactly as displayed before
  power-cycling.
- The rating plate is usually inside the door frame on laundry and
  dishwashers, and on an interior side wall on refrigerators.
- Electrolux front-load washers and matching dryers are common in stacked
  installations — if a caller reports new vibration or banging, ask whether
  the stacking kit and feet have been checked before assuming a component
  failure.

## Care notes

- Run the washer's clean cycle periodically and wipe the door gasket dry;
  leave the door ajar between loads.
- Clean the dishwasher filter monthly and check spray arms for debris.
- Vacuum refrigerator condenser coils about twice a year and keep clearance
  around heat-venting appliances, per the general maintenance tips guide.
