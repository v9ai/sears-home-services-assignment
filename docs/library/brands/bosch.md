# Bosch appliance care and service notes

Original brand-oriented guidance (not sourced from any manufacturer manual).
Nothing here overrides the safety interrupt or the deterministic troubleshooting
trees in `app/knowledge/*.yaml`.

## About the brand at Sears

Bosch is a European brand best known in the US for quiet dishwashers, and yes —
Sears sells and services Bosch appliances: dishwashers first and foremost, plus
compact laundry, refrigeration, ranges, wall ovens, and cooktops.

## Service notes

- Bosch dishwashers are notably quiet; callers sometimes report a "dead"
  machine that is actually mid-cycle. Have them check for a running-time or
  status indicator (on many models projected onto the floor or shown on the
  door edge) before assuming a failure.
- Bosch error codes are usually an "E" followed by digits; ask the caller to
  read the code exactly as displayed.
- Many Bosch dishwashers heat water internally and run longer, cooler cycles
  than US-style machines — a longer cycle time is normal, not a defect.
- The rating plate is typically on the inner door edge of dishwashers and
  inside the door frame on compact laundry.

## Care notes

- Clean the dishwasher filter assembly (bottom of the tub) monthly — a
  clogged filter is the most common cause of poor wash results and standing
  water on any Bosch dishwasher.
- Compact Bosch condensation dryers need their condenser and lint filters
  cleaned regularly; they don't use an exterior vent, so airflow maintenance
  happens inside the machine.
