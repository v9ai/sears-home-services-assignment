# Kenmore appliance care and service notes

Original brand-oriented guidance (not sourced from any manufacturer manual).
Nothing here overrides the safety interrupt or the deterministic troubleshooting
trees in `app/knowledge/*.yaml`; it's background and care advice for callers with
Kenmore units.

## About the brand at Sears

Kenmore is the Sears house brand and the most common badge on appliances Sears
Home Services is asked to repair. Sears sells and services Kenmore washers,
dryers, refrigerators, freezers, dishwashers, ranges, ovens, cooktops,
microwaves, water heaters, and HVAC equipment, including the higher-end Kenmore
Elite and Kenmore Pro lines.

## Model numbers matter more than usual

Kenmore appliances are built by several different manufacturers, and the first
three digits of the model number identify which one actually made the unit. That
prefix is the single most useful thing a caller can read off the rating plate:
it tells the technician which parts family the machine belongs to before the
truck rolls. Ask callers to read the full model number exactly as printed —
including the digits before and after the period (for example, a dishwasher
model like 665.13743K310).

## Care notes

- The rating plate is usually inside the door frame (laundry, dishwashers,
  ranges) or on the interior side wall (refrigerators).
- Kenmore laundry pairs benefit from the same routine as any washer/dryer:
  wipe the door gasket after heavy use, and keep the dryer's exterior vent
  clear — see the general maintenance tips guide.
- If a Kenmore unit shows a fault code on its display, note it exactly as
  shown (letters and digits) before calling; codes differ across the
  manufacturers behind the brand, so the exact string plus the model prefix is
  what makes it actionable.
