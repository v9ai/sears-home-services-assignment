# Frigidaire appliance care and service notes

Original brand-oriented guidance (not sourced from any manufacturer manual).
Nothing here overrides the safety interrupt or the deterministic troubleshooting
trees in `app/knowledge/*.yaml`.

## About the brand at Sears

Frigidaire (an Electrolux family brand) is a refrigeration-first name that
Sears sells and services: refrigerators, freezers, dishwashers, ranges, wall
ovens, microwaves, laundry, and room air conditioners. Frigidaire and
Electrolux units frequently share parts families.

## Service notes

- Frigidaire units often show short letter/digit error codes on the control
  panel; ask the caller to read the code exactly as displayed before resetting.
- The rating plate is typically on an interior side wall or ceiling of
  refrigerators and freezers, inside the door frame on dishwashers and
  laundry, and behind the door or drawer on ranges.
- On chest and upright freezers, a report of "everything thawed" should
  include whether the compressor is running at all and whether the power
  indicator is lit — it narrows the visit considerably.

## Care notes

- Vacuum refrigerator and freezer condenser coils about twice a year; on many
  Frigidaire models they're behind the lower front grille.
- Keep door gaskets clean and check that doors self-close and seal — a fridge
  that "runs constantly but won't get cold" often starts with a seal problem.
- For window and room air conditioners, wash or replace the filter at the
  start of each season and keep the outdoor-facing coil unobstructed.
